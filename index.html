Condensed Matter Theory

Qiang Gao

Postdoctoral Fellow · Harvard University

I am a postdoctoral fellow in physics at Harvard University, following my Ph.D. from The University of Texas at Austin in 2023. My research centers on condensed matter theory, with core interests in quantum optics, Floquet systems, moiré materials, and quasi-periodic order.
