Research

My research interests are primarily the following:

  • Topological band theory and (fractional) quantum Hall effect
  • Advanced numerical method for solving many-body physics
  • Time-periodically driven system
  • Moiré materials and moiré-induced quasi-periodic order

Notable works done so far:

  • Proposed the oblique space-time crystal, the Floquet-Bloch backaction, and the Floquet quasisteady acoustoelectric effect
  • Formulated the semi-classical theory for Floquet-Bloch electrons
  • Proposed a model for realizing the Fractional Chern insulator in moiré-layer graphene
  • Applied the bootstrap principle to the quantum Hall system

I am also open to new fields of study especially:

  • Quantum information and computation
  • Non-hermitian quantum system
  • Machine learning in physics
